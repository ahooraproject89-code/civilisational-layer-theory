## Core Discoveries & Concepts

1. **The Bifurcation Law:** Every technological revolution throughout history splits the population into two stable attractors: a stronger elite (via voluntary practice and mastery) and an atrophied majority (via delegation to technology).
2. **The Compression Law & The 2043 Fixed Point:** A derived mathematical model demonstrating that all civilisational layer transitions are exponentially compressing, converging toward a stable singularity point around the year **2043 CE**.
3. **The Variance-Collapse Theorem in AI:** A formal proof showing that recursive AI-on-AI training inevitably leads to a collapse in output variance ($\sigma_n \rightarrow 0$). Civilisational creativity will freeze without continuous "variance injection" from human error and agency.
4. **Epistemic Dependency Disorder (EDD):** The predicted psychological pathology of the new era. It acts as the cognitive equivalent of Type-2 Diabetes, caused by the over-delegation of volitional goal-setting to AI agents.
